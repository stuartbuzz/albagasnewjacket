Alba Gas Innovations - PlugSafe®

With the recent addition of Part P to the building Regulations, safety issues have been raised and recorded regarding safe isolation in domestic premises for all trades.

PlugSafe will enable domestic trade types such as Gas, Electric, Plumbing etc. as well as non electrically competent DIY enthusiasts to work in a quick, safe, confident and competent manner safe the knowledge that the appliance is isolated locally or remotely without the need to remove Plug tops and or Plug top Fuses.

PlugSafe was originally created for Gas Central Heating Service Engineers who were working on Appliances powered by a 3 pin Plug top. There was a need for them to carry out a safe isolation process before commencing work. Unfortunately, this meant that in order safely isolate to required standards, the Engineers had to execute a sometimes complex and laborious process before work commencement.

In most circumstances, the Engineers only ever isolated the appliance by removing the plug top from the Socket irrespective of plug top location before proving safe isolation and working.

Again most Engineers did not remove the plug top fuse and retain then apply warning notice. . This would not be classed as safe working as the fuse would not be removed and the chance of accidental power up would always be there.

This put both Engineers and the Customer at risk.

All of our research proved that most Tradesmen felt that their works current safe isolation process was too complex or time consuming.

From these simple beginnings of finding a solution for Gas Service Engineers, Plug Safe has expanded to meet the market demand. In particular, the Health and Safety divisions of various companies are keen to put the emphasis of simple safe isolation back onto the Engineer.

Various other industries have also expressed an interest in the need for a product that proves their appliance has not been used. This would retain the warranty for specialised commissioning purposes.

PlugSafe® Technical data

Weight : 18.2 grams at room temperature.

Material Type : Made from 1.5 mm thick bonded Polypropylene plastic making the unit flexible, durable and hardwearing. The Living hinge mechanism has been tested to 1600 continual bends before fracture.

Dimensions : Small at 700mm x 600mm x 30mm, easily stored in toolbox.

Warning Notice : A distinguishable label attached in a sticker form. Reduces the need for clumsy oversized warning notices. Note: This is an additional payment and has to be requested.

Safe Isolation : PlugSafe uses standard tie wraps to lock off or standard miniature lock and keys.

PlugSafe® User Guide

  • Verify circuit to be isolated.
  • Inform Customer.
  • Remove Plug top from Socket.
  • Remove and retain Fuse. (If required)
  • Open front cover of Plug Safe.
  • Place Plug Safe over Plug top.
  • Fold Cover up into a closed position.
  • Apply Danger Work in Process Warning Label. (D00123)
  • Apply pad lock. (A20 Brass Padlock 3mm C00-068)
  • Retain key.
  • Verify safe Isolation on Circuit to be worked on.
  • Prove Electrical Testing Instrumentation integrity.
  • Work safely.

Under what circumstances would you commonly use PlugSafe?®

  • Safe isolation of plug tops to avoid accidental power up of appliances and systems.
  • Safe isolation of plug tops to avoid unwanted appliance usage.
  • Safe isolation of plug tops to ensure appliance validity for commissioning purposes.
