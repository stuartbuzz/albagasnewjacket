Alba Gas Installation Overview

All of our Installations are done to the same high standard irrespective of price. We include a lot more in the installation than your average Installer.

All of our Installations are done to current Building Regulations and the Domestic Heating Compliance Guide.

Please remember that the sweet taste of that good deal you got, soon turns to bitter dissatisfaction after the work is done badly. We have no doubt you could get cheaper, but why take that risk?

All of Gas Engineers are Corgi Registered with full ACS Qualifications and all Electricians are NICIEC Registered.

All of these deals are only subject to Technical Survey.
