Welcome to the Alba Gas homepage.

It is my privilege, on behalf of all staff at Alba Gas to present to you our Website for you information.

Alba Gas was proudly established in 2004 supplying Corporate and Private Customers alike with high quality Gas and Electrical workmanship.

Based in Govan, Glasgow, our Management Team and Engineers consists of professional people whose careers have been immersed in the Gas and Electrical industry for a collective number of years.

Our business ethics are firmly based upon hard work and a fair pricing structure to rival any other companies nationally.

Alba Gas grows to meet demand.

Alba Gas has enjoyed yet another excellent year of trading in 2007, with high standards being set thank to the unstinting efforts of all our staff.

It has been an extremely busy recently for Alba Gas. Our work force has increased by 50% since 2006 whist maintaining a high Engineer retention rate without compromising quality.

All 2006 business targets have been surpassed. We relocated to larger premises and our new Work Management system gives greater ability to cope with our business needs.

We have also had a very successful past 6 months with the introduction of our Electrical Division fronted by Electrical Manager Les McPherson.

For the future we are expanding each individual business stream and continue to add to our business portfolio. For further information please continue to view our Website for regular updates.
