Alba Gas Quality Background...

The Alba Gas Senior Management Team has gained a wealth of experience from over a collective 60 years working in the Gas Industry and Electrical Industries.

Both David Liddell Operations Manager and Daniel Shaw General Manager have over 20 years experience in Gas Training within British Gas. All of our Gas Engineers are Qualified to NVQ Level 1 or 2 in Domestic Natural Gas Maintenance/Installation.

All Gas Engineers have completed full ACS Certification in all appliances. This includes all Quality assessors/Quality Auditors and Senior Management.

All Electricians are fully Qualified and registered.

All Engineers undergo continuous Internal Quality Assurance checks by Alba Gas QAE's and External Quality Audit's by British Gas who are in a partnership with. The majority of our work load is for British Gas.

Alba Gas regularly updates all Engineers on any Gas or Electrical Industry changes through Training Sessions.

All of our employees are fully qualified in their relevant trades. We are Gas Registered through CORGI and NICEIC Registered for all Electrical Work.
