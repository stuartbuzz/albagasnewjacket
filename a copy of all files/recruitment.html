Gas Applicants must possess:

  • Current ACS certificates in CCN1 and CEN1 and 4 appliances
  • Full clean driving license
  • Full clean disclosure (criminal record check)
  • Relevant experience

Solid Fuel Applicants must possess:

  • Hetas certification under building regulations 200 schedule 2A
  • Full clean driving license
  • Full clean disclosure (criminal record check)
  • Relevant experience

Oil Applicants must possess:

  • OFT 101
  • OFT 105E
  • OFT 600A
  • Oftec certification
  • Full clean driving license
  • Full clean disclosure (criminal record check)
  • Relevant experience

Electrical Applicants must possess:

  • CSCS
  • BS7671 Inspection and Testing
  • IEE 17th Edition
  • Full clean driving license
  • Full clean disclosure (criminal record check)
  • Relevant experience

Plumbing Applicants must possess:

  • City and Guilds Standards
  • Full clean driving license
  • Full clean disclosure (criminal record check)
  • Relevant experience

Working for the public at large we do carry out a CRB check on all applicants.

Want to work for Alba Gas?

At Alba Gas we consider ourselves a people company and believe that our biggest asset is our staff, so we are continually looking for career minded individuals who want to join our expanding team of experienced engineers.

We fully support all our Engineers with our ongoing training and development program, allowing them to realise their true potential.

As a company we value honesty and integrity and expect all our staff to treat clients and customers, as they themselves would wish to be treated.

We value the opinions of all our team members and operate a high level of communication throughout the company.

As an expanding company we are looking for the following qualified personnel from all over Scotland, who can meet our standards.

Job Roles

  • Heating Engineers
    Engineers have to be able to undertake installation and commissioning of new multi-fuel central heating systems and/or replacement of central heating boilers and Long Duration work in all aspects of Heating Installation.
  • Servicing Engineers
    Engineers have to be able to undertake regular servicing of Gas and preferably other fuel types central heating installations and related servicing work across both full Appliance strip downs and Flue Performance Analyser Testers.
  • Diagnostic Engineers
    Engineers have to be able to undertake diagnostic Fault Finding on a large range of Gas appliances. We would expect a high ratio of first time fixes with low recall rate or incorrect part rate.
  • Electricians
    Engineers have to be able to undertake all Domestic Electrical work.
  • Plumbers
    Engineers have to be able to undertake all Domestic Plumbing work including full Bathroom suites to Wet Flooring.
  • Quality Assurance Engineers
    Required to have achoeved D31 & D32 or A1 & A2 City in Guilds (Assessor). Domestic Energy Assessor (Level 3) or NHER Site assessor equivalent. Part L building standard Energy efficiency Qualified (England & Wales).
